Chapter 1  Introduction to use

Main interface and main functions

Function description:

Navigation

Provide navigation reference;

Audio Player

Support WMA, MP3and WAV format audio files;

Support random play, sequence play, circle play;

Photo Browser

JPG, PNG, TIFF formats photos browsing;

Support photo rotation, zoom in/ out, auto play all photos.

E-book

Txt format text reading, support page selection;

Video Player

Play .mov, .asf and .avi format video files;

Support progress adjustment, suspend and full-screen play;

Setup

Volume, power, backlight, date and time, language, system information.
